Our Mission & Legacy

More than a decade of frontline conservation. We've dedicated our lives to protecting the world's most vulnerable species through community action and scientific rigor.

Explore Our Journey Our Missions

Our Mission

To end extinction by empowering local leaders and deploying revolutionary field technologies in the world's most vulnerable habitats.

Our Vision

A world where majestic wildlife and human progress thrive in perpetual harmony, supported by transparent and actionable conservation.

The Heart of the Savanna

Founded in 2012, Wildlife began as a small troop of rangers in East Africa. Today, we manage over 15 million hectares of land across three continents. Our approach is unique: we don't just protect animals; we build economies around their survival.

Our Journey

2012: The Foundation

Started by Dr. Elena Vance with just 5 rangers and a dream to stop ivory poaching.

2015: Tech Expansion

Deployed the first satellite-linked GPS collars to track elephant migraton in real-time.

2018: Global Coalition

Partnered with 20 nations to create the 'Amazon Corridor' project.

2023: Impact Threshold

Surpassed 1 million trees planted and 50 endangered species back from the brink.
